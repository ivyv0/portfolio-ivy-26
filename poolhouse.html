Poolhouse Arcade

A personal Blender project focused on building a cinematic interior scene with neon lighting, dark atmosphere, material studies and spatial composition.

Blender 3D Lighting
Type

Personal 3D Project

Focus

Lighting / Materials

Tool

Blender

Context

Poolhouse Arcade is a personal 3D project I created in Blender to practise building a complete interior scene. I wanted to explore how lighting, materials and composition can create a specific mood inside a digital space.

Visual Direction

The scene is inspired by dark arcade rooms, neon lighting and cinematic interior photography. The goal was to create a space that feels atmospheric, slightly mysterious and visually rich, while still being readable as a room.

A key reference for the atmosphere was a complete room scene with an arcade machine. I used this as inspiration for the lighting setup, material choices and overall composition: a dark environment with glowing light sources, reflective surfaces and a strong focal point.

Process

I experimented with the placement of the arcade machine, the poolhouse setting, neon accents and camera angle. I focused on balancing the darker parts of the room with coloured light, so the scene would feel cinematic instead of flat.

Material settings were also important. I explored how different surfaces react to light, especially in a darker scene where reflections and glow can define the mood of the space.

Outcome

The final render presents a complete 3D room with an arcade machine, designed around atmosphere, lighting and composition. This project helped me practise scene building in Blender and understand how small visual choices can change the feeling of a digital environment.
